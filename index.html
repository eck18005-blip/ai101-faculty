AI 101 for Faculty

Elevating AI Literacy Across BYU-Idaho

Course Vision

AI 101 for Faculty exists to rapidly elevate AI literacy among faculty so they can teach with and make informed decisions about AI in ways that advance learning and professional effectiveness.

Learning Outcomes Weekly Schedule Course Structure Instructional Team
Express Interest

The Benefits

Earn ½ hour of Professional Development Leave (PDL)
Develop a personal ethical framework for using AI in teaching and professional work
Make sound, gospel-centered decisions about AI, integrity, and student learning
Leave with ready-to-use AI syllabus statements for your courses
Learn to leverage AI tools to support your academic, professional, and personal productivity
Analyze how AI is impacting your discipline and industry
Build confidence to integrate AI intentionally into learning experiences
